                Regulatory Disclosure
            

            
Philippine Regulatory Compliance Framework

            
Quanby Legal operates in full compliance with Philippine law and applicable regulatory standards governing electronic notarization, digital identity, data privacy, and fintech services.


            
            

                
 Supreme Court of the Philippines

                

                    

                        
A.M. No. 24-10-14-SC

                        
Rules on Electronic Notarization in the Philippines. Quanby Legal is built in full compliance with this Supreme Court issuance, supporting both In-Person (IEN) and Remote (REN) electronic notarization nationwide.

                        sc.judiciary.gov.ph ↗
                    

                    

                        
2004 Rules on Notarial Practice

                        
All notarial acts performed through Quanby Legal adhere to the 2004 Rules on Notarial Practice as amended and extended by A.M. No. 24-10-14-SC to cover electronic notarial instruments.

                    

                    

                        
Electronic Notarization Facility (ENF)

                        
Quanby Legal operates as a registered ENF provider. All Electronic Notary Publics (ENPs) on our platform have completed SC-mandated identity verification and training requirements.

                    

                

            


            
            

                
🏛️ DICT & E-Commerce Legislation

                

                    

                        
RA 8792 — E-Commerce Act

                        
Electronic documents and signatures processed through Quanby Legal carry full legal weight under the Electronic Commerce Act of 2000, ensuring validity and enforceability of digitally notarized instruments.

                    

                    

                        
RA 8792 — Electronic Signature Standards

                        
All electronic signatures on our platform conform to DICT-prescribed electronic signature standards, including PKI-based certificates issued by the Philippine National Public Key Infrastructure (PNPKI).

                        DICT PNPKI ↗
                    

                    

                        
DICT National PKI — Identity Verification

                        
All ENPs are verified through the Philippine National PKI operated by DICT, ensuring government-grade identity assurance for every notary on the Quanby Legal platform.

                    

                

            


            
            

                
 Data Privacy (NPC / RA 10173)

                

                    

                        
RA 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012

                        
Quanby Legal is compliant with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR). We have appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) and maintain a Personal Information Management System (PIMS).

                        National Privacy Commission ↗
                    

                    

                        
NPC Registration

                        
Quanby Solutions, Inc. is registered as a Personal Information Controller (PIC) with the National Privacy Commission. All personal data is processed lawfully, transparently, and with appropriate security safeguards.

                    

                    

                        
Data Retention & Security

                        
All notarized documents are encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3. Blockchain-anchored audit trails (via DOCONCHAIN / Hyperledger) ensure immutability. Data retention periods follow NPC guidelines.

                    

                

            


            
            

                
🏦 BSP — Financial & Fintech Compliance

                

                    

                        
BSP Circular No. 944 — eKYC Standards

                        
Our identity verification workflows align with BSP Circular No. 944, which sets standards for electronic Know-Your-Customer (eKYC) processes used by BSP-supervised financial institutions. This ensures Quanby Legal is interoperable with banks and EMIs that rely on notarized documents.

                    

                    

                        
BSP Circular No. 1108 — Digital Onboarding

                        
Electronic notarization services provided through Quanby Legal support compliant digital customer onboarding under BSP Circular No. 1108, enabling financial institutions to accept e-notarized documents for account opening and lending.

                    

                    

                        
AML & AMLA Awareness

                        
Quanby Legal implements internal controls aligned with the Anti-Money Laundering Act (RA 9160, as amended). ENPs are trained to flag suspicious transactions and we maintain records compliant with AMLC reporting requirements.

                    

                

            


            
            

                
📋 SEC — Corporate & Securities Compliance

                

                    

                        
SEC Memorandum Circular No. 28-2020 — Remote Notarization

                        
Quanby Legal supports corporate transactions compliant with SEC MC No. 28-2020 (later extended), which authorized remote notarization for SEC filings and corporate acts during and beyond the pandemic-era digital transition.

                    

                    

                        
SEC Memorandum Circular No. 6-2021 — Digital Corporate Records

                        
Documents notarized through Quanby Legal are accepted for SEC submissions under SEC MC No. 6-2021, covering GIS, AFS, and other mandatory corporate filings that require notarization.

                    

                    

                        
Corporate Governance

                        
Quanby Solutions, Inc. is duly registered with the Philippine SEC. We submit required annual reports and maintain corporate governance standards per the Revised Corporation Code (RA 11232).
